Photography

Capturing Time

Photography is one of my oldest hobbies, and one I've started making money with on the side. I've been doing it for about 6 years on and off, with very little formal education. I started back when my Grandmother gave me her old camera, and I would be in her garden taking pictures of all her flowers, and bugs that would reside on the flowers. Since then I've spread out in the things I shoot. always looking for more opportunities to learn. It's become a backbone to whenever I'm stuck with school, sports, or anything else when I need to take a mental reset.
